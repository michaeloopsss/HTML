Sasaki Kojirō

Sasaki Kojirō was a Japanese swordsman. He was born in Fukui Prefecture in 1585. In Japanese history, he was considered a master of his craft during the Sengoku Period and the early years of the Edo Period . Toda Seigen and Kanemaki Jisai are believed to have introduced Sasaki Kojirō to the Chūjō-ryū style of swordsmanship . He was known to be skilled in the use of nodachi . He traveled throughout the provinces after defeating his master's younger brother.




"The way of the sword is the way of life. It is not a game."
